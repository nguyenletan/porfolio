## Live site

- [KidsFirst](https://www.kidsfirstaustralia.org.au//)


### About

Kids First is a leading provider of education, family, and support services for children and families in Melbourne. Kids First have over 120 years in the childcare industry. In 2019, Kids First revamped its website to give it a new, professional, and modern look. They also wanted to improve its speed by using GatsbyJs - an open-source static website generator (SSG).

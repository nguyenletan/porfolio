### About

- Marqui CMS is a web content management platform serving small marketing teams to large multi-site organizations with various pages and users.

- Marqui CMS provides website authoring, editing and administration in a way that is less programming or code intensive. This allows business users and non technical people to build and maintain websites without information technology department support or input. Most feature templates, scalability, Web standard compliance, collaboration, versioning, workflow managment, content repositories, SEO capability and multilingual support.
